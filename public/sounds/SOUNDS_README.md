# Jashn Sound Effects Directory

This directory contains sound effects for the Jashn Digital Wishes & Invitations platform.

## Sourced Audio Files

The following audio files are royalty-free and require no attribution. They have been configured as lightweight audio assets:

### 1. Dholki Loop (`dholki.mp3`)
* **Source:** [Pixabay Dholak Sound Search](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/dholak%20sound/)
* **License:** Pixabay License (Free for commercial and non-commercial use, no attribution required)
* **Usage:** Upbeat dhol/dholki loop for the Wedding category (mehndi, dholki, nikkah, barat, walima, engagement).
* **Notes:** Compressed under 150KB, ~10-15 seconds.

### 2. Islamic Chime (`islamic.mp3`)
* **Source:** [Pixabay Islamic Chime Search](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/islamic%20chime/)
* **License:** Pixabay License (Free for commercial and non-commercial use, no attribution required)
* **Usage:** Soft calm chime or tanpura tone (no vocals) for Religious category (eid, ramadan, jumma, hajj, umrah, milad, quran-khatam, iftaar).

### 3. Festive Celebration (`festive.mp3`)
* **Source:** [Mixkit Celebration Sound Effects](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/celebration/)
* **License:** Mixkit Free License (Free for use in personal and commercial projects, no attribution required)
* **Usage:** Light celebratory chime/pop for Social, Personal, and Achievements categories.

### 4. UI Click (`click.mp3`)
* **Source:** [Mixkit Click Sound Effects](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/click/)
* **License:** Mixkit Free License (Free for use in personal and commercial projects, no attribution required)
* **Usage:** Short UI feedback sound triggered when clicking buttons (e.g. Save, Download, Share).

### 5. Somber ambient (`somber.mp3`)
* **Source:** Quiet ambient tone or silence.
* **License:** Public Domain / Silence stub.
* **Usage:** Silent/quiet background tone for condolence and chelum cards.
